Premier Wealth total rewards

It is our pleasure to introduce a distinguished Premier Wealth Client like yourself to the privileges of our new total rewards programme.

With total rewards, you can be assured that your banking relationship with us will be given due recognition. The more transactions you make, the more you will accelerate your accumulation of TREATS Points.

Be recognised whenever you save, invest, insure or borrow with us, plus when you charge to your Premier Wealth World MasterCard or Premier Wealth Platinum Debit Card. With accelerated TREATS Points of up to 200,00 per month, it’s a more rewarding financial relationship.
