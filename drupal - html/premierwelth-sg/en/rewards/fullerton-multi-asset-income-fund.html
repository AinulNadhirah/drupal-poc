Fund details

Fullerton Total Return Multi-Asset Income (the “Fund”) invests primarily in global growth stocks and Asian bonds for capital growth and regular payouts. The Fund adopts a total return approach and is flexibly positioned to respond to uncertainties and capitalise on market opportunities for higher growth.

Why invest in the fund?

  • High conviction stocks with strong growth potential
    Seeks to invest in businesses globally that benefit from structural changes, or with business models that potentially provide sustained earnings growth.
  • Regular dividend payout
    Seeks to achieve a stable monthly payout from both underlying income and capital gains.
  • Flexible global allocation
    Dynamically captures quality opportunities in stocks and bonds across geographies and sectors.
